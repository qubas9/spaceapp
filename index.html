This website was created as a apart of the Nasa Space Apps Challenge, "Will It Rain on My Parade?". The goal of this website is to provide users with a reliable tool to give them an idea of the weather conditions at a certain point in the future based on a Month and Day. This information is presented based on data from NASA sources and provided API.
