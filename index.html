About Us (Click)

This website was created as part of the NASA Space Apps Challenge, "Will It Rain on My Parade?".

The goal of this website is to provide users with a reliable tool to give them an idea of the weather conditions at a certain point in the future based on a month and day. This information is presented based on data from NASA sources and provided APIs.
